About MUSA

The pillars of MUSA include, Unity, Vision & Progress

  • Vision

    • To make Makueni shine in producing competent and reliable graduates.

  • Mission

    • To build and strengthen peaceful interactions within the society through youth empowerment trainings, awareness creation, advocacy and collaborations.

  • Aim

    • The Association seeks to unite students, alumni and professionals and achieve a point where they will view diversity in ideologies as a positive aspect of the development. It seeks to strengthen the spirit of brotherhood among the members.

Services

MUSA prides itself in collaborating with students and other stakeholders to offer the following services.

Mentorship

Previously, MUSA has held mentorship sessions mainly for high school students as part of our service to the community.

Environmental Conservation

Environmental conservation is one of our key tasks, we seek to plant plants as part of our chapter level projects in collaboration with other members of the society.

Bursaries

MUSA liaises with the county government of Makueni and the respective offices of the members of the national assembly to secure bursaries for comrades. Members of MUSA also constitute the Makueni county bursary advisory committee.

Education Financial Support

MUSA members and other individuals of goodwill contribute to needy students who need fee support.

Partners

MUSA works with the county government of Makueni, nonprofits and other organizations. Our partners contribute more than money. Their ideas, volunteer power, in-kind support and more are helping build a stronger MUSA.
